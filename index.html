About

As a passionate devotee to signal processing, I am constantly driven by the intricate challenge of metamorphosing raw data into cogent information. My scholarly pursuits are deeply rooted in the domain of Implicit Neural Representation, a discipline that offers boundless opportunities and complex challenges. Moreover, my intellectual curiosity extends to various compression techniques, some of which I had exploring during the CLIC22. The ever-evolving arena of digital technology has always presented captivating methodologies such as Super-Resolution (SR) and de-noising. These sectors not only intrigue me but also motivate my ambitions to pioneer, innovate, and devise revolutionary solutions that can recalibrate our understanding and interpretation of signals.
